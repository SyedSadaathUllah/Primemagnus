Structured hospital operations improve performance and reduce revenue loss.

PMCG works with hospitals to identify breakdowns in patient flow, utilisation and execution, and fix them through structured operational and governance interventions.

How PMCG Works

PMCG works through four core areas to improve hospital performance:

  • •Patient Flow – Assess how patients move from OPD to admission and discharge and identify where conversion is lost.
  • •Process – Review workflows across departments and remove operational bottlenecks.
  • •Performance – Improve utilisation of beds, OTs, diagnostics and revenue capture.
  • •People & Accountability – Align teams, roles and ownership so decisions translate into action.

What We Do Not

PMCG does not operate referral networks, patient commission models or volume-based corridors.

Our work is governance-led, operational and focused on institutional performance.
