PMCG Services

PMCG helps hospitals improve performance by identifying where patients, processes and decisions break down and fixing them through structured operational work.

All services are built around four core areas: patient flow, process, performance, and people & accountability.

Hospital Performance Diagnostic

A focused assessment of where performance and revenue are being lost.

  • •Patient flow (OPD to IP conversion)
  • •Capacity utilisation (beds, OTs, diagnostics)
  • •Process breakdowns across departments
  • •Revenue leakage and case progression gaps

Clear findings and a structured action plan.

Operational Improvement & Execution

Hands-on support to fix breakdowns identified in the diagnostic.

  • •Improving patient conversion and movement
  • •Fixing coordination between clinical and admin teams
  • •Strengthening workflows and escalation pathways
  • •Supporting implementation of changes

Focus is on execution, not advisory.

Governance & Clinical Readiness

Independent assessment and strengthening of hospital readiness.

  • •Clinical readiness and documentation standards
  • •Accreditation and compliance alignment
  • •Governance frameworks and oversight structures
  • •Operational discipline and accountability systems

Healthcare Advisory & Due Diligence

Support for investors and healthcare organisations evaluating hospitals or expansion opportunities.

  • •Operational performance review
  • •Risk and governance assessment
  • •Hospital capability evaluation
  • •Market and adoption readiness insights

Cross-Border Care & IPP

Selective support for hospitals building structured international patient capability.

  • •International Patient Programme (IPP) design
  • •Clinical and operational pathway alignment
  • •Referral governance and documentation standards
  • •Integration with hospital operations

Delivered only in select engagements.
